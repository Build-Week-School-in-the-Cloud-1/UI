School in the Cloud

About Us

School in the Cloud is a platform that focuses on the development of volunteer that seek to become mentors and give back to their community. Our goal is to provide the best mentorship experience to those students who feel lost during their studies. To achieve this we plan very carefully the skill path our volunteer needs to complete before they are ready to be posted on the platform for students to start scheduling study sessions with them. Also, this is just a project to deliver to school and it's required to move on, but shh you didn't hear this here. School in the Cloud takes very seriously the development of the students that is the reason why the volunteers need to go through a filter to be able to become mentors.
